* **Improving Profitability:** Net profit recovered to ₹ 43,851 Cr in March 2026 from a low of ₹ 35,262 Cr in March 2025, supported by a reduction in interest expenses to ₹ 6,904 Cr in the same period.
* **Institutional Investor Support:** Domestic Institutional Investors (DIIs) have steadily increased their holding from 13.78% in March 2020 to 20.46% in March 2026, showing continued confidence in the company.
